FAILURE MODE TO RECOGNIZE (the whole point of the design)
If the promotion gate raises in stage 2, the job turns red THERE:
stages 3 and 4 never execute, blue keeps 100% of traffic, and the
promotion-report artifact (uploaded with if: always()) explains why.
Rollback is not an action — it is the abstention from promotion.
