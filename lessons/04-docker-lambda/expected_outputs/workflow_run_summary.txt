FAILURE MODES TO RECOGNIZE
- RIE smoke test red with grep finding nothing: the image built but the
  handler errored — docker logs in the step output tell you why
  (most common: artifacts/ missing because training was skipped).
- deploy red at "Assume role": the OIDC trust policy sub condition does
  not match your repo/ref — re-read docs/aws_setup.md step 3.
- deploy red at update-function-code: the deploy role's permissions
  policy does not cover the function ARN — step 3 again.
